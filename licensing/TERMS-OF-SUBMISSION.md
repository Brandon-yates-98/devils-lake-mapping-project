# Terms of Submission, Community Content (DRAFT)

> ⚠️ **DRAFT skeleton, NOT legal text.** Community uploads involve other
> people's copyright, photos of people, and GPS/EXIF data, so this **must** be
> drafted/reviewed by an attorney and paired with a Privacy Policy before any
> uploads are accepted. The app already stores EXIF lat/lng
> (`pending_images`), so privacy applies from day one.

When you submit photos, descriptions, or other content ("Submission"):

## 1. You keep ownership; you grant a license
You retain copyright in your Submission. You grant the project a **worldwide,
royalty-free, non-exclusive, sublicensable, irrevocable license** to host,
store, reproduce, adapt, display, and distribute the Submission in connection
with the project **and its noncommercial custom experiences** (provided as
volunteer gifts per `PERMITTED-USES.md`).

> *Decision point:* if you also want Submissions to be shareable **upstream**
> (e.g., to Wikimedia/OSM), offer contributors an option to release under
> **CC-BY-SA 4.0** or **CC0**, note those permit commercial reuse and therefore
> cannot also be "noncommercial-only."

## 2. Your warranties
You represent that you created the Submission (or have the rights to submit it),
that it doesn't infringe anyone's rights, and that you have consent from
identifiable people shown.

## 3. Privacy
Submissions may include location/EXIF metadata. See the **Privacy Policy**
[link TBD] for what is collected, displayed, and retained, and how to request
removal.

## 4. Moderation & takedown (DMCA)
The project may review, edit, or remove Submissions. Copyright owners may
request removal via the project's designated **DMCA agent** [contact TBD];
infringing or policy-violating content will be taken down.

## 5. No compensation
Submissions are voluntary; no wages or payment are owed for them (consistent
with the project's noncommercial model).
